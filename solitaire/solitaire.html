Pairs

Rules:- Pick a pair of cards. If the pair is of the same rank (number) and same color (either red or black) then you win that pair, else they are again put face down. Play again till you all the pairs have been picked up. Objective is to reduce the amount of flips needed to find all the pairs in as little time as possible. The fun part of the game is memorization and focus, so don't make a note of the position of cards apart from your memory.
